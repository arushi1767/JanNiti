## What is this scheme
Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is an old-age income security scheme for savings account holders aged 18-40 who are not income-tax payers. It addresses longevity risks among unorganised-sector workers and encourages voluntary retirement saving. It mainly targets the poor, the under-privileged and workers in the unorganised sector.

## Why it was introduced
To give unorganised-sector workers a guaranteed pension in old age and encourage them to save voluntarily for retirement.

## Benefits
- Guaranteed minimum pension of Rs. 1,000 / 2,000 / 3,000 / 4,000 / 5,000 per month from age 60 until death (subscriber chooses the slab).
- After the subscriber's demise, the spouse receives the same pension until death.
- After both die, the nominee receives the accumulated pension wealth (corpus as at age 60).
- Contributions are eligible for tax benefit under section 80CCD(1), like NPS.
- Voluntary exit before 60: refund of own contributions plus net actual accrued income (minus account maintenance charges). Government co-contribution (pre-31.03.2016 joiners) is not refunded.
- Death before 60: spouse may continue the account until the subscriber would have turned 60 and then draw the same pension — or take the entire accumulated corpus.

## Eligibility
- Age 18 to 40 years at joining; pension starts at 60.
- Must hold a savings bank / post office account with auto-debit enabled.
- Contributions via auto-debit monthly, quarterly or half-yearly, from joining until age 60.

## Exclusions
- From 1 October 2022, any citizen who is or has been an income-tax payer cannot join APY.

## Required Documents
- KYC details are fetched from your active bank / post office savings account (Aadhaar is the primary KYC for identifying beneficiary, spouse and nominee).
- Aadhaar / mobile for OTP-based eKYC if enrolling online via eNPS.

## How to Apply
Online (net banking): log in to internet banking, search APY on the dashboard, fill basic and nominee details, consent to auto-debit, submit.
Online (eNPS): visit enps.nsdl.com, select "Atal Pension Yojana" > "APY Registration", complete KYC via Aadhaar OTP / offline XML / Virtual ID, choose pension amount and contribution frequency, fill nominee details, eSign via Aadhaar OTP.
Offline: visit your bank/post office branch and submit the APY registration form.

## Important Dates
- No deadline — enrol any time between ages 18 and 40. Contributions continue till age 60.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Income-tax payers (from 01.10.2022) are barred — if found later, the account can be closed. [red]
- Charges, fees and overdue interest apply for delayed/non-payment of contributions, as prescribed by PFRDA. [yellow]
- Keep enough balance for auto-debit; repeated failures attract overdue interest. [yellow]
- Grievances: raise free of cost at npscra.nsdl.co.in (NPS-Lite / CGMS) and track with the token number. [green]
