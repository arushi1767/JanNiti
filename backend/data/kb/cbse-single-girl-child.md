# CBSE Merit Scholarship Scheme for Single Girl Child (SGC)
ministry: Ministry of Education (implemented by CBSE)
apply: https://www.cbse.gov.in/cbsenew/scholar.html
official: https://www.cbse.gov.in/cbsenew/scholar.html
mode: online
aliases: cbse single girl | single girl child scholarship | sgc | cbse merit scholarship

## What is this scheme
Launched by the Ministry of Education and implemented by CBSE, this scheme promotes education among single girl children and encourages meritorious students to continue studies. Financial assistance goes to single girl students in classes 11 and 12 who excelled in the CBSE class 10 examination and study in CBSE-affiliated schools.

## Why it was introduced
To promote girls' education and recognise families observing the small family norm.

## Benefits
- Rs. 1,000 per month per student for a maximum of 2 years (classes 11 and 12).
- Disbursed directly via ECS/NEFT.
- Valid until completion of class 12, subject to renewal conditions.

## Eligibility
- Single girl child of her parents (twins/all girls born together also count as single girl child).
- 70% or more marks in the CBSE class 10 examination.
- Studying in class 11 or 12 in a CBSE-affiliated school.
- Tuition fee not exceeding Rs. 2,500/month in class 10, and not exceeding Rs. 3,000/month in classes 11-12.
- Gross parental/family income not exceeding Rs. 8,00,000 per annum.
- Indian national; must continue studies in classes 11 and 12.
- NRI students eligible with tuition fee up to Rs. 6,000/month.

## Required Documents
- Passport-size photograph and scanned signature.
- Aadhaar card.
- Admission proof.
- Student's ID card.
- Class 10 mark sheet (class 11 mark sheet for renewal cases).
- Aadhaar-linked bank account details.
- Fee receipt verified by the school principal.
- Income affidavit on Rs. 50 stamp paper (self-declaration by parent), attested by Gazetted Officer / SDM / First-Class Magistrate.

## How to Apply
1. Visit the CBSE scholarship page (cbse.gov.in/cbsenew/scholar.html).
2. Click "New User Registration" (or "Renewal").
3. Read the instructions, continue, enter your class 10 roll number and date of birth.
4. Print the undertaking from the Guidelines, fill it, paste your photo and get it attested by the school; make the affidavit per the Guidelines.
5. Scan the undertaking and affidavit as PDFs (max 1 MB, all pages included) and upload.
6. Print the confirmation page (renewals: courier the signed confirmation to Assistant Secretary (Scholarship), CBSE, Shiksha Kendra, 2 Community Centre, Preet Vihar, Delhi - 110092).
Note: the application must be verified by the school; maintain good conduct and regular attendance.

## Important Dates
- Application windows are notified yearly on the CBSE scholarship page — check for the current session's dates.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Tuition-fee caps are strict: over Rs. 2,500/month in class 10 or Rs. 3,000/month in 11-12 makes you ineligible. [red]
- Family income cap Rs. 8 lakh/year, proven by an attested affidavit. [red]
- School verification and regular attendance are mandatory; renewals need the class 11 mark sheet. [yellow]
