# Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)
ministry: Ministry of Finance (implemented via Department of Food & Public Distribution)
apply: https://nfsa.gov.in
official: https://nfsa.gov.in
mode: offline
aliases: pmgkay | garib kalyan | anna yojana | free ration | garib kalyan anna

## What is this scheme
PMGKAY, part of Atmanirbhar Bharat, supplies free food grains to migrants and the poor. The Centre provides 5 kg of free food grain per person per month, in addition to the subsidised (Rs. 2-3/kg) ration under the National Food Security Act (NFSA) for families covered by the Public Distribution System (PDS). It ran in phases from April 2020; Phase VI (April-September 2022) entailed an estimated additional food subsidy of about Rs. 80,000 crore.

## Why it was introduced
To protect poor and migrant families from food insecurity, especially during and after the COVID-19 period.

## Benefits
- 5 kg free food grain per month per person for ration-card-holding families — over and above the subsidised PDS grain.
- Wheat allocated to Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Chandigarh, Delhi and Gujarat; rice to the remaining States/UTs.
- One Nation One Ration Card lets NFSA beneficiaries draw entitlements from anywhere in India.

## Eligibility
- Families in the Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) and Priority Households (PHH) categories.
- PHH identified by State Governments/UTs; AAY per central criteria, including: households headed by widows, terminally ill or disabled persons, or persons 60+ with no assured means of subsistence; single women/men with no support; all primitive tribal households; landless agricultural labourers, marginal farmers, rural artisans (potters, tanners, weavers, blacksmiths, carpenters), slum dwellers and informal daily earners (porters, coolies, rickshaw pullers, fruit/flower sellers, rag pickers, cobblers, destitutes); BPL families of HIV-positive persons.

## Required Documents
- Ration Card.
- Aadhaar Card (if seeded with the Ration Card).

## How to Apply
There is no separate application. Visit your nearest Fair Price Shop with your Ration Card:
- Quote your ration card number or Aadhaar number to any Fair Price Shop dealer across the country.
- Authenticate via Aadhaar fingerprint or iris identification and collect the grain.

## Important Dates
- Distribution is monthly through Fair Price Shops.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Only AAY and PHH ration-card families qualify — a general ration card is not enough. [red]
- Grain type (wheat/rice) depends on your State/UT allocation. [green]
- With One Nation One Ration Card, migrants can draw rations in any state. [green]
