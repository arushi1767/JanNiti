## What is this scheme
The National Talent Scholarship (NTS) is implemented by ICAR under "Strengthening and Development of Higher Agricultural Education in India". It is awarded to students pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Agriculture and allied disciplines who secured admission through the All India Entrance Examination (AIEE) and study in an ICAR-funded Agricultural University OUTSIDE their state of domicile. Initially awarded for one year; renewable for the remaining degree duration subject to academic performance and conduct.

## Why it was introduced
To support meritorious agricultural students who move outside their home state for study, promoting national integration in agricultural education.

## Benefits
- Rs. 3,000 per month during the Bachelor's degree programme.
- Note: no contingency grant is provided.

## Eligibility
- Pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Agriculture or allied disciplines.
- Admission secured through ICAR's AIEE.
- Enrolled in an Agricultural University outside your state of domicile.
- The university must be funded under the ICAR scheme.

## Required Documents
- Recent passport-size photograph.
- Signature.
- Thumb impression.
- Admission proof through AIEE.
- Aadhaar number.
- Aadhaar-linked bank account details.
- Any other documents prescribed by ICAR or the university.

## How to Apply
Application is made online through the National Scholarships Portal process:
1. New registration on NSP (mobile OTP + eKYC); note the reference number.
2. Generate OTR ID via the NSP OTR app with Aadhaar FaceAuth.
3. Log in, fill the application form, upload documents, Final Submit.
4. Track payments on PFMS "Know Your Payment".

## Important Dates
- NSP notifies opening/closing dates yearly; renewals must be filed before the cut-off.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules (continuation conditions)
- Minimum OGPA/CGPA of 7.00/10 at end of first year (6.50 for SC/ST) — else scholarship stops. [red]
- Must not take any other scholarship, any employment (even part-time), change discipline or change university, or leave within one month of admission. [red]
- Regular attendance required; participation in strikes/agitations that disrupt studies can cancel the award. [yellow]
- Must clear all papers each semester in regular examinations. [yellow]
