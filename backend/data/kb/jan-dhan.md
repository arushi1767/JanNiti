# Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)
ministry: Ministry of Finance
apply: https://www.pmjdy.gov.in
official: https://www.pmjdy.gov.in
mode: offline
aliases: pmjdy | jan dhan | jan dhan yojana | jandhan

## What is this scheme
PMJDY is the National Mission for Financial Inclusion launched in August 2014 to provide universal banking services for every unbanked household — banking the unbanked, securing the unsecured, funding the unfunded. From 14.08.2018 the focus shifted from "every household" to "every unbanked adult".

## Why it was introduced
To give weaker sections and low-income groups access to a basic savings account, need-based credit, remittances, insurance and pension.

## Benefits
- Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA) with NO minimum balance requirement (cash withdrawals limited to four per month).
- Small Account / Chota Khata can be opened without full legal documents (valid 12 months, extendable 12 more if you show you've applied for an Officially Valid Document).
- Free RuPay debit card with inbuilt accident insurance cover of Rs. 2 lakh (Rs. 1 lakh for accounts opened before 28.08.2018).
- Overdraft facility up to Rs. 10,000 (raised from Rs. 5,000).
- Banking through branches, ATMs and Business Correspondents (Bank Mitras) in rural areas.

## Eligibility
- Indian nationals.
- Minors above 10 years can open an account with support of a legal guardian.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar.
- Government ID proof (Voter Card / PAN Card / Ration Card).
- Permanent address proof (Passport / Driving Licence / electricity, telephone or water bill).
- Passport-size photograph.
- Filled and signed PMJDY account-opening form.
- Any other document notified by the Central Government.

## How to Apply
1. Go to the official PMJDY portal (pmjdy.gov.in).
2. Under "e-Documents", download the Account Opening Form (English or Hindi) and print it.
3. Fill it manually — bank branch, town/village, block/district, Aadhaar number, profession, annual income, KCC details, etc.
4. Visit your nearest bank branch and submit the form with the documents and your mobile number/email.
This scheme is applied for at the bank branch — the portal only provides the form and information.

## Important Dates
- Open-ended — accounts can be opened any time.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- A Small Account (opened without documents) expires after 12 months unless you show proof of applying for a valid ID. [red]
- BSBDA allows only four cash withdrawals per month. [yellow]
- The RuPay card accident cover applies subject to card-usage conditions set by NPCI/banks. [yellow]
