# Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)
ministry: Ministry of Finance
apply: https://www.jansuraksha.gov.in
official: https://www.jansuraksha.gov.in
mode: hybrid
aliases: pmjjby | jeevan jyoti | jeevan jyoti bima | life insurance yojana

## What is this scheme
PMJJBY is a one-year life insurance cover, renewable year to year, for death due to ANY reason. It is offered by banks/post offices and administered through life insurance companies. Account holders of participating banks/post offices aged 18 to 50 can join.

## Why it was introduced
To give low-cost life insurance protection to ordinary bank account holders, especially those outside formal insurance.

## Benefits
- One-year term life cover of Rs. 2 lakh for subscribers aged 18-50.
- Covers death due to any reason.
- Premium: Rs. 436 per annum, auto-debited from the subscriber's bank/post office account.

## Eligibility
- Age between 18 and 50 years.
- Must hold an individual bank / post office account.

## Required Documents
- Duly filled and signed Consent-cum-Declaration / Enrolment form (download: https://www.jansuraksha.gov.in/Files/PMJJBY/English/ApplicationForm.pdf).
- Self-attested KYC copy — per the official form: Aadhaar card, or Voter ID (EPIC), or MGNREGA card, or Driving Licence.
- Active savings bank / post office account with auto-debit consent.
- Nominee details.

## How to Apply
Offline:
1. Download and print the Consent-cum-Declaration form from jansuraksha.gov.in.
2. Fill and sign it, attach self-attested KYC copies, and submit to the authorised official of your bank / post office.
3. You receive an "Acknowledgement Slip cum Certificate of Insurance".
Online: many banks allow enrolment via net banking / mobile banking under Insurance > Social Security Schemes.

## Important Dates
- Cover period runs 1 June to 31 May every year; premium is auto-debited annually.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- A 30-day lien (waiting) period applies from enrolment: natural death in the first 30 days is not covered (accidental death is covered from day one). [red]
- You can enrol through only ONE bank account; multiple enrolments get restricted to Rs. 2 lakh and extra premium can be forfeited. [yellow]
- Keep sufficient balance for the annual auto-debit or the cover lapses. [yellow]
