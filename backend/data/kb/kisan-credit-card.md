# Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
ministry: Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
apply: https://www.jansamarth.in/kisan-credit-card-scheme

## What is this scheme
The KCC Scheme provides adequate and timely credit to farmers for their agricultural operations through a single window with simplified procedures. The Government of India provides 2% interest subvention and 3% Prompt Repayment Incentive, making credit available at an effective rate of about 4% per annum. It also covers investment credit for allied and non-farm activities.

## Why it was introduced
To meet farmers' short-term credit needs for crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, produce marketing loans, household consumption, working capital for farm assets and allied activities, and investment credit for agriculture and allied activities.

## Benefits
- Credit at an effectively subsidised rate of ~4% p.a. (2% interest subvention + 3% prompt repayment incentive).
- First-year short-term limit: scale of finance for the crop x area cultivated + 10% for post-harvest/household needs + 20% for repairs/maintenance of farm assets + crop/PAIS/asset insurance.
- Limit increases 10% every successive year (up to the 5th year); term loans for land development, minor irrigation, farm equipment and allied activities.
- A RuPay/ATM-enabled card usable at ATMs, micro-ATMs, PoS at input dealers and via mobile banking.

## Eligibility
- Farmers — individual/joint borrowers who are owner cultivators.
- Tenant farmers, oral lessees and share croppers.
- Self Help Groups (SHGs) or Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) of farmers including tenant farmers and share croppers.

## Required Documents
- Application form.
- Two passport-size photographs.
- ID proof — Driving Licence / Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport.
- Address proof — Driving Licence / Aadhaar Card.
- Proof of landholding duly certified by the revenue authorities.
- Cropping pattern (crops grown) with acreage.
- Security documents for loan limits above Rs. 1.60 lakh / Rs. 3.00 lakh, as applicable.
- Any other document as per sanction.

## How to Apply
1. Visit the website of the bank you wish to apply to (or the Jan Samarth portal's KCC page).
2. Choose Kisan Credit Card from the options and click "Apply".
3. Fill the form with the required details and submit.
4. An application reference number is issued; if eligible, the bank contacts you within 3-4 working days.
Final verification and sanction is completed at your bank branch.

## Important Dates
- No fixed deadline; apply any time through your bank.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Interest subvention applies to prompt repayment — delay and you lose the 3% incentive, so the loan becomes costlier. [red]
- For loan limits above Rs. 1.60 lakh (Rs. 3.00 lakh where applicable) the bank can demand collateral security. [yellow]
- The card limit is split into sub-limits for short-term credit and term loans, each with different interest rates and repayment schedules. [yellow]
- Banks have discretion to adapt the scheme to their location and customer base — exact terms vary by bank. [green]
