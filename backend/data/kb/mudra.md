# Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)
ministry: Ministry of Finance
apply: https://www.udyamimitra.in
official: https://www.mudra.org.in
mode: online
aliases: mudra | pmmy | mudra loan | mudra yojana | shishu | kishore | tarun

## What is this scheme
PMMY facilitates micro credit up to Rs. 20 lakh to income-generating micro enterprises in the non-farm sector (manufacturing, trading, services) including allied-agriculture activities like poultry, dairy and beekeeping. Loans are extended by Member Lending Institutions: public/private banks, cooperative banks, RRBs, MFIs, NBFCs and Small Finance Banks.

## Why it was introduced
To fund the unfunded — small proprietorships, shopkeepers, vendors, truck operators, food-service units, repair shops, artisans and other micro units that lack formal credit.

## Benefits
- Shishu: loans up to Rs. 50,000.
- Kishore: above Rs. 50,000 and up to Rs. 5 lakh.
- Tarun: above Rs. 5 lakh and up to Rs. 10 lakh.
- Tarun Plus: up to Rs. 20 lakh for entrepreneurs who successfully repaid a Tarun loan.
- Upfront fee/processing charges for Shishu loans are waived by most banks. Interest rates set by lenders per RBI guidelines.

## Eligibility
- Individuals, proprietary concerns, partnership firms, private/public limited companies and other legal forms.
- Applicant must not be a defaulter to any bank/financial institution and should have a satisfactory credit record.
- Individual borrowers may need necessary skills/experience/knowledge for the proposed activity; educational need is assessed per the activity.

## Required Documents
For Shishu:
- Identity proof (self-attested): Voter ID / Driving Licence / PAN / Aadhaar / Passport / Government-issued photo ID.
- Residence proof: recent telephone/electricity bill or property tax receipt (not older than 2 months) / Voter ID / Aadhaar / Passport / bank passbook or attested statement / domicile certificate.
- 2 recent colour photographs (not older than 6 months).
- Quotation of machinery/items to be purchased; supplier name, machinery details, prices.
- Business proof: licences / registration certificates / ownership-address documents of the enterprise, if any.
For Kishore, Tarun and Tarun Plus, additionally:
- Statement of accounts for last 6 months from existing banker, if any.
- Last two years' balance sheets with income tax / sales tax returns (loans Rs. 2 lakh and above).
- Projected balance sheet for 1 year (working capital) or loan period (term loan) (Rs. 2 lakh and above).
- Sales achieved in the current financial year up to application date.
- Project report with technical and economic viability.
- Memorandum & Articles of Association / Partnership Deed; asset-liability statement of borrower/directors/partners if no third-party guarantee.

## How to Apply
1. Go to the PM MUDRA site (mudra.org.in) and select the Udyamimitra portal (udyamimitra.in).
2. Click Mudra loan "Apply Now"; choose New entrepreneur / Existing entrepreneur / Self-employed professional.
3. Register with name, email, mobile + OTP.
4. Fill personal and professional details; optionally pick hand-holding agencies, else go to "Loan Application Center".
5. Select loan category (Shishu/Kishore/Tarun), fill business information, owner details, credit facilities, future estimates and preferred lender.
6. Attach documents and submit; note the Application Number.
Mudra loans can also be applied through Jan Samarth and directly at bank branches.

## Important Dates
- No deadline — apply any time.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- There are NO agents or middlemen for MUDRA loans — anyone claiming to be one is a fraud. [red]
- Defaulters to any bank/FI are not eligible. [red]
- Interest rates vary by lender — compare before applying. [yellow]
