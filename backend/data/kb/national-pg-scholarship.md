# National Scholarship for Post Graduate Studies (NSPG)
ministry: Ministry of Education
apply: https://scholarships.gov.in

mode: online
aliases: nspg | national pg scholarship | post graduate scholarship | pg scholarship

## What is this scheme
NSPG, established by the Ministry of Education, provides financial assistance to meritorious students from economically weaker sections to pursue postgraduate studies in India.

## Why it was introduced
To make postgraduate education affordable for meritorious students from economically weaker sections.

## Benefits
- Rs. 15,000 per month for 10 months a year.

## Eligibility
- Pursuing the FIRST postgraduate degree (those who already completed a PG degree are not eligible).
- Enrolled in the first year of the PG programme.
- Under 30 years of age.
- Enrolled full-time in a recognised PG degree programme.

## Required Documents
- Attested copies of mark sheets and certificates from class 9 onwards.
- Attested copy of the provisional/final PG admission letter.
- Proof of identity (Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / PAN / Driving Licence).
- Bank details.
- Two recent passport-size photographs.

## How to Apply
1. New registration on the National Scholarships Portal: accept terms, mobile OTP verification, eKYC; note the reference number.
2. Generate OTR ID via the NSP OTR app + AadhaarFaceRD (eKYC with FaceAuth).
3. Log in with OTR + password, fill the application form, upload documents, "Final Submit" (or save draft).
4. Track payment on PFMS "Know Your Payment".
Verification happens at two levels: your institute, then the State Higher Education Department / State Nodal Agency.

## Important Dates
- NSP announces opening and closing timelines each year. Renewal applications must be submitted before the cut-off — missing it forfeits that year's renewal (you are not permanently debarred).

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Second PG degrees are not funded. [red]
- Missing the renewal cut-off loses that year's scholarship. [red]
- Two-level verification (institute + state) — chase both if payment is delayed. [yellow]
