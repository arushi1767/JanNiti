# Post Graduate Indira Gandhi Scholarship for Single Girl Child (PG-IGSSGC)
ministry: Ministry of Education (UGC)
apply: https://scholarships.gov.in
official: https://www.ugc.gov.in/page/Scholarships.aspx
mode: online
aliases: indira gandhi scholarship | pg single girl | pg-igssgc | ugc single girl child

## What is this scheme
A UGC scholarship for girl students admitted to the first year of PG courses in designated universities/PG colleges who are the only girl child in their family. Fellowship: Rs. 36,200 per annum for the full duration of a PG course (2 years). 3,000 fresh scholarships are awarded each year, paid annually via DBT. Applications are made online on the National Scholarship Portal after UGC's notice.

## Why it was introduced
- To support postgraduate education of single girl children.
- To recognise the value of observing small family norms and compensate the direct costs of girls' education.

## Benefits
- Rs. 36,200 per annum for two years (full PG duration).
- 3,000 fresh scholarships/year, DBT mode, annual payment.
- Note: no additional grant for hostel or medical charges.

## Eligibility
- Citizen of India; the only girl child in the family (girls without brothers; twin/fraternal daughters may also apply).
- Age not more than 30 years at the time of first-year PG admission.
- Admitted to a regular, full-time first-year master's course in a designated university/PG college.
- Non-professional course, with regular classes at a recognised institution.
- Not through distance education; PG first-year students only.

## Exclusions
- Distance-education PG admission is not covered.
- Deemed universities that don't receive Central/State government funds are not eligible institutions.
- If a family has one son and one daughter, the girl is NOT considered a single girl child.

## Required Documents
Student:
- Aadhaar card.
- Proof of admission to a recognised, UGC-recognised master's course in India.
- Joining report (Annexure-I format).
- Affidavit of single girl child status (Annexure-II format).
- Copy of bank passbook.
- Birth certificate or class 10 mark sheet.
Institution (post-award):
- Progress report with marks after first year; funds utilisation certificate.

## How to Apply
1. New registration on the National Scholarships Portal (NSP): accept terms, verify mobile with OTP, complete eKYC; note your reference number.
2. Generate an OTR (One Time Registration) ID: install AadhaarFaceRD + the NSP OTR app, do eKYC with FaceAuth using the reference number.
3. Fresh application: log in with OTR + password on NSP, complete the application form, upload documents and "Final Submit".
4. Track payment via PFMS "Know Your Payment" using Aadhaar / bank account / NSP application ID.

## Important Dates
- Applications invited once a year on NSP; short notifications on the UGC website. Incomplete applications are summarily rejected.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- One brother in the family disqualifies the applicant. [red]
- Distance-mode PG is excluded. [red]
- Only PG first-year students may apply; the affidavit and joining report formats are mandatory. [yellow]
