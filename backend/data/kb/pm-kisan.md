# Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
ministry: Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare

## What is this scheme
PM-KISAN, implemented by the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers' Welfare, supplements the financial needs of all landholding farmers' families. Rs. 6,000 per year is released by the Central Government online, directly into the bank accounts of eligible farmers under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT), subject to certain exclusions.

## Why it was introduced
To supplement the financial needs of landholding farmers' families for buying farm inputs (to ensure proper crop health and yields) and for domestic needs.

## Benefits
- Rs. 6,000 per year per eligible family, paid in 3 equal installments of Rs. 2,000 every four months.
- Money goes directly to the farmer's bank account (DBT) — no middlemen.

## Eligibility
- All landholding farmers' families that have cultivable land holding in their names.

## Exclusions
- All institutional land holders.
- Families where any member is/was a holder of a constitutional post.
- Former/present Ministers, MPs, MLAs, MLCs, Mayors of Municipal Corporations, Chairpersons of District Panchayats.
- Serving or retired officers/employees of Central/State Government, PSEs and attached/autonomous institutions, and regular employees of local bodies (excluding Multi Tasking Staff / Class IV / Group D).
- Retired pensioners with monthly pension of Rs. 10,000 or more (excluding MTS/Class IV/Group D).
- Anyone who paid Income Tax in the last assessment year.
- Practising professionals — doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants, architects registered with professional bodies.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar Card.
- Landholding papers (land record documents).
- Savings bank account details.

## How to Apply
Online (also via CSCs):
1. Visit the PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in).
2. Click "New Farmer Registration".
3. Enter your Aadhaar number and mobile number.
4. Enter the OTP to authenticate your Aadhaar with UIDAI.
5. Fill the required information and upload the mandatory documents.
6. Click "Submit". You can check status later via "Know Your Status" on the same portal.

## Important Dates
- Installments are released every four months (3 per year). No fixed application deadline — registration stays open.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Income-tax payers in the family make the whole family ineligible — even if they own farmland. [red]
- The land must be in the applicant's own name; cultivating someone else's land does not qualify. [yellow]
- Bank account must be Aadhaar-seeded, otherwise installments fail. [yellow]
