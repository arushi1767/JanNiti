# Stand-Up India
ministry: Ministry of Finance
apply: https://www.standupmitra.in
official: https://www.standupmitra.in

aliases: stand up india | standup india | stand-up india | sc st women loan

## What is this scheme
Stand-Up India facilitates bank loans between Rs. 10 lakh and Rs. 1 crore to at least one Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) borrower AND at least one woman borrower per bank branch, for setting up a greenfield enterprise in manufacturing, services, trading or allied-agriculture. For non-individual enterprises, at least 51% shareholding and controlling stake must be with an SC/ST or woman entrepreneur.

## Why it was introduced
To promote entrepreneurship among SC/ST communities and women by guaranteeing them access to bank credit for first-time (greenfield) ventures.

## Benefits
- Composite loan (term loan + working capital) between Rs. 10 lakh and Rs. 100 lakh.
- RuPay debit card issued for borrower convenience.
- SIDBI's Stand-Up Mitra portal provides hand-holding: training, skill development, mentoring, project report preparation, application filling, work-shed/utility support and subsidy scheme guidance.

## Eligibility
- Finance is for greenfield (first-time) enterprises only.
- Male applicants must be from SC/ST category; women of any category are eligible.
- Minimum age 18 years.
- Applicant must not be in default to any bank/financial institution.

## Required Documents
- Identity proof: Voter ID / Passport / Driving Licence / PAN / banker's signature identification (proprietor, partner or director).
- Residence proof: recent telephone/electricity bill, property tax receipt / Passport / Voter ID.
- Proof of business address.
- Proof the applicant is not a defaulter to any bank/FI.
- Memorandum & Articles of Association / Partnership Deed.
- Assets & liabilities statement of promoters and guarantors with latest income tax returns.
- Rent agreement (if premises rented) and pollution control board clearance if applicable.
- SSI/MSME registration if applicable.
- Projected balance sheets (2 years for working capital; loan period for term loan).
- Photocopies of lease/title deeds of properties offered as primary/collateral security.
- SC/ST category proof, where applicable; Certificate of Incorporation from ROC to establish 51% SC/ST/Woman stake.
For exposure above Rs. 25 lakh, additionally: unit profile (promoters, directors, activity, offices/plants, shareholding), last 3 years' balance sheets of associate/group companies, detailed project report (machinery, suppliers, prices, capacity, projections, staffing) and manufacturing/competition profile.

## How to Apply
- Approach your nearest bank branch, or
- Go through the Lead District Manager (LDM) of your district (list at standupmitra.in/LDMS), or
- Apply on the portal: standupmitra.in > Register — enter business location; category (SC/ST/Woman, 51%+ stake); nature of business, desired loan, premises details; past business experience; hand-holding preference; personal and enterprise details; then Register. The bank then contacts you to complete the loan process.

## Important Dates
- No deadline — apply any time.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Only GREENFIELD ventures qualify — expanding an existing business does not. [red]
- Male applicants must be SC/ST; general-category men are not eligible. [red]
- For companies, SC/ST/Woman must hold at least 51% and control. [yellow]
- Defaulters to banks/FIs are excluded. [yellow]
