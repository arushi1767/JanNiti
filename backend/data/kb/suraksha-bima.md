# Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
ministry: Ministry of Finance
apply: https://www.jansuraksha.gov.in
official: https://www.jansuraksha.gov.in
mode: hybrid
aliases: pmsby | suraksha bima | accident insurance yojana | suraksha bima yojana

## What is this scheme
PMSBY is an accident insurance scheme offering accidental death and disability cover. Premium is just Rs. 20 per annum, auto-debited from the account holder's bank/post office account on or before 1 June each coverage year. Coverage is valid one year, 1 June to 31 May.

## Why it was introduced
To give very low-cost accident insurance to bank account holders, particularly economically vulnerable citizens.

## Benefits
- Death due to accident: nominee gets Rs. 2 lakh.
- Total, irrecoverable loss of both eyes, or both hands/feet, or one eye plus one hand/foot: Rs. 2 lakh.
- Total, irrecoverable loss of sight of one eye or use of one hand/foot: Rs. 1 lakh.

## Eligibility
- Individual bank account holders of participating banks aged 18 (completed) to 70 (age nearer birthday) who consent to auto-debit.

## Required Documents
- Duly filled PMSBY application/consent form (download: https://jansuraksha.gov.in/Forms-PMSBY.aspx).
- KYC identity proof — Aadhaar is the primary KYC for the bank account (submit a copy if not already linked).
- Active savings bank / post office account with auto-debit consent.
- Nominee details.

## How to Apply
Offline:
1. Visit the bank branch where you hold a savings account, or download the form from jansuraksha.gov.in/Forms-PMSBY.aspx.
2. Fill the form and submit it with the required documents to the bank.
3. You receive an Acknowledgement Slip cum Certificate of Insurance.
Online: via your bank's net banking (Insurance > Social Security Schemes) or SMS-based enrolment where offered.

## Important Dates
- Cover period 1 June to 31 May; premium auto-debited on/before 1 June every year.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- Cover terminates at age 70 or on account closure / insufficient balance for auto-debit. [red]
- If covered through more than one account, cover is restricted to Rs. 2 lakh. [yellow]
- Only accidents are covered — natural death or illness is NOT covered (that is PMJJBY's job). [yellow]
