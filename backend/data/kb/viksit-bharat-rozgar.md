# Pradhan Mantri Viksit Bharat Rozgar Yojana (PM-VBRY)
ministry: Ministry of Labour and Employment (implemented by EPFO)
apply: https://pmvbry.epfindia.gov.in
official: https://www.epfindia.gov.in
mode: online
aliases: pm-vbry | vbry | viksit bharat rozgar | rozgar yojana | employment incentive

## What is this scheme
PM-VBRY promotes generation of sustained additional employment, workforce formalisation and employability. It gives financial assistance to first-time employees and incentivises eligible establishments (especially manufacturing) for creating jobs above their baseline. Implemented by EPFO; applications are accepted continuously during the registration period 01.08.2025 to 31.07.2027 through online establishment updates and employee Aadhaar authentication.

## Why it was introduced
To encourage employers to create additional formal jobs and support first-time employees through their learning curve.

## Benefits
Part A — First-Timer employees:
- One-time incentive up to Rs. 15,000 to first-time employees.
- 1st installment: up to Rs. 7,500 (half of average EPF wage of six continuous completed months).
- 2nd installment: average of 12 completed months' EPF wage minus the 1st installment, capped so total is Rs. 15,000; kept in a savings instrument/deposit for a specified period.
- Paid via DBT (Aadhaar Bridge) to Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts, within 45 days of eligibility + paid ECR filing.
Part B — Employers:
- Incentives for 2 years for employment above baseline (4 years for manufacturing).
- EPF wage <= Rs. 10,000: up to Rs. 1,000/month per additional employee, plus 10% of EPF wage as risk-mitigation for wages below Rs. 10,000.
- EPF wage > Rs. 10,000 up to Rs. 20,000: Rs. 2,000/month.
- EPF wage > Rs. 20,000 up to Rs. 1,00,000: Rs. 3,000/month.
- Paid six-monthly (lump sum) after six completed months' ECRs, via DBT to the establishment's PAN-linked bank account.

## Eligibility
Employees (First Timers):
- Not previously a contributing EPFO / Exempted Trust member before scheme commencement.
- Join an establishment during 01.08.2025-31.07.2027.
- UAN authenticated by Face Authentication on the UMANG app.
- Gross wages up to Rs. 1,00,000/month at joining.
- ECRs with contributions filed for six continuous months; seasonal-industry employees must stay with the same establishment the full six months.
Employees (Re-joinees):
- Previous EPF contributory membership; join an EPFO-registered establishment/exempted trust in the registration period; Aadhaar-authenticated UAN.
Establishments:
- Covered under the EPF & MP Act, 1952; unique PAN; regular ECR filing with contributions.
- Generate at least 2 additional jobs (baseline < 50) or at least 5 (baseline >= 50).
- Provide PAN, GSTN and PAN-linked bank account details.

## Exclusions
- Employees who joined before 01.08.2025.
- Establishments with pending Section 7A/7B/7C inquiries under the EPF & MP Act.
- Establishments with an EPFO-filed FIR for fraudulent practices.
- Pending/decided irregularity inquiries under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Rojgar Yojana.

## Required Documents
Application/registration stage:
- Unique PAN details of the establishment.
- GSTN details.
- Registration Certificate of the establishment (to verify manufacturing status).
Benefit disbursal stage:
- Monthly ECR with prescribed contributions.
- PAN-linked bank account details (establishment disbursement).
- Aadhaar-seeded bank account details (employee disbursement).
- Aadhaar-authenticated UAN details.
- Face Authentication (FAT) registration on the UMANG app (for first-timers).

## How to Apply
Establishments: access the EPFO portal, complete deemed registration, and update PAN, GSTN and PAN-linked bank account for DBT.
Employees: download the UMANG app, generate/activate UAN, and complete Aadhaar Face Authentication.
Benefits: establishment files monthly ECRs with contributions; six completed wage months trigger the automated six-monthly benefit calculation and disbursement.
Post-application: first-timers must complete the mandatory online Financial Literacy Course on the EPFO employee portal to receive the 2nd installment; grievances via EPFO's online redressal (resolution within 15 days).

## Important Dates
- Registration period: 01.08.2025 to 31.07.2027.
- Employee incentive disbursed within 45 days of eligibility; employer benefits six-monthly.

## Hidden Conditions & Rules
- The 2nd employee installment is paid ONLY after completing the Financial Literacy Course on the EPFO portal. [red]
- Employer benefits stop for any month the additional-employment threshold isn't met (2 jobs if baseline <50; 5 if >=50). [red]
- First-timers in seasonal industries must stay with the same establishment for the full six months. [yellow]
- UAN must be Face-Authenticated on UMANG — without it no benefit flows. [yellow]
