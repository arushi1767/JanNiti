Details
The “CBSE Merit Scholarship Scheme for Single Girl Child (SGC)”
scheme has been launched by the Ministry of Education, Government of
India, and implemented by the Central Board of Secondary Education.
The scheme aims to promote education among single girl children and
encourage meritorious students to continue their studies. Under this
scheme, financial assistance is provided to single girl students studying
in classes 11th and 12th. It supports students who have demonstrated
academic excellence in class 10th examinations and are enrolled in
CBSE-affiliated schools.

Benefits

Financial Assistance

1,000 per month per student provided for a maximum period of 2 years
(classes 11th and 12th)

Mode of Disbursement

Direct transfer through ECS (Electronic Clearing Service) / NEFT

(National Electronic Funds Transfer)

Validity

Up to completion of class 12 (maximum 2 years), subject to renewal
conditions
þÿ
Eligibility
The applicant should be a single girl child of her parents.
The applicant should have secured 70% or more marks in CBSE class
10th examination.
The applicant should be studying in Class 11 or 12 in a CBSE-affiliated
school.
The applicant should have a tuition fee not exceeding 2,500 per month

in class 10th.

The applicant should have a tuition fee not exceeding 3,000 per month

in classes 11th and 12th.

The applicant should have gross parental/family income not exceeding

8,00,000 per annum.

The applicant should be an Indian National.

The applicant should continue her studies in classes 11th and 12th.

All children born together (such as twins) are considered a single girl
child.

NRI students are eligible and their tuition fee should not exceed 6,000

per month.

py

Application Process

Online

The interested applicant visits the CBSE official website.

Click on ‘New user Registration’ or ‘Renewal’.

Read all the instructions on the official website and then hit 'Continue.'
After that, fill in the roll number, and DOB (as mentioned class 10 mark

sheet). Now click on ‘Continue’.

Print the undertaking mentioned on the ‘Guidelines’ document. After
that, fill it out, paste the photo, and get the same attested from school.
Make an affidavit as mentioned in the ‘Guidelines’.
Now, scan the undertaking and affidavit. The files must be in PDF
format, not exceeding 1 MB. Please note that the PDF must contain all
pages.
Finally, select ‘Upload Document’ to upload both of these documents.
After that, print the confirmation page.
Please Note: Only in case of a successful application, a confirmation
page will appear. In case of renewal, courier the duly filled and signed
confirmation page to the below address: Assistant Secretary
(Scholarship), CBSE, Shiksha Kendra, 2 Community Centre, Preet
Vihar, Delhi - 110 092.
NOTE:
The applicant’s application must be verified by the school.
The applicant must maintain good conduct and regular attendance.
