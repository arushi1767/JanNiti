Details
The scheme "Pradhan Mantri Viksit Bharat Rozgar Yojana" was
launched by the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of
India. The scheme aims to promote the generation of sustained
additional employment, increase the formalization of the workforce, and
enhance employability. The scheme provides comprehensive financial
assistance to first-time employees to navigate their learning curve and
incentivizes eligible establishments, particularly in the manufacturing
sector, for generating sustained additional employment over their
established baseline. The applicant must regularly file the Electronic
Challan-cum-Return along with provident fund contributions and fulfill
the defined threshold criteria for new employment generation. The
scheme is implemented by the Employees' Provident Fund
Organisation. The applications for this scheme are accepted
continuously during the registration period from 01.08.2025 to
31.07.2027 through the online updating of establishment details and

employee Aadhaar authentication on the official portal.

Benefits

Part A: First-Timer Benefits

The scheme provides a one-time incentive of up to 15,000/- to assist

first-time employees.

The first installment is up to a maximum of 7,500/-, calculated as half
of the average Employees’ Provident Fund wage for six continuous

completed months.

The second installment is equal to the average of the twelve completed
months' Employees' Provident Fund wage reduced by the first

installment, restricted to a total incentive of 15,000/-.

The second installment will be kept in an appropriate saving instrument

or deposit account for a specified period.

The incentives to the employees shall be paid through Direct Benefit
Transfer using the Aadhaar Bridge Payment System to their

Aadhaar-seeded bank accounts.

The incentive shall be disbursed within 45 days after fulfilling the

eligibility condition and filing of the paid Electronic Challan-cum-Return.

py

Part B: Support to Employers

The scheme provides incentives to the employer for two years in

respect of employment generated over and above the baseline.

For the manufacturing sector, the incentive to the employer will continue

for the third and the fourth year.

For additional employees with an Employees' Provident Fund wage less
than or equal to 10,000/-, the benefit to the employer is up to 1,000/-

per additional employment per month.

In respect of employees getting Employees' Provident Fund wages
below 10,000/-, the incentive at the rate of 10 percent of the
Employees' Provident Fund wage will be provided as an additional risk

mitigation measure.

For additional employees with an Employees' Provident Fund wage
greater than 10,000/-andupto 20,000/-, the benefit is 2,000/- per

additional employment per month.

For additional employees with an Employees' Provident Fund wage
greater than 20,000/-andupto 1,00,000/-, the benefit is 3,000/- per

additional employment per month.

The benefits will be provided to all eligible establishments on a
six-monthly lump-sum basis after the filing of six completed months'

Electronic Challan-cum-Returns.

The incentive to the establishment shall be provided as a Direct Benefit
Transfer to the Permanent Account Number-linked bank account of the

establishment.

py

Conditions

The employer must fulfill the threshold limit continuously, generating at
least 2 additional employments with a baseline of less than 50, and at
least 5 additional employments with a baseline of 50 or above.
The first-time employee must successfully complete the mandatory
Financial Literacy Course available through the Employees' Provident
Fund Organisation Employee portal to receive the second installment.
The benefits to the employer will not accrue for any month in which the
employer is not eligible as per the threshold criteria laid out in the
Scheme.

Eligibility
Eligibility for Employees (First Timers)
The applicant must not have been a contributing member of the
Employees' Provident Fund Organisation or an Exempted Trust prior to

the commencement of the scheme.

The applicant must join an establishment during the scheme registration
period from 01.08.2025 to 31.07.2027.

The applicant must have their Universal Account Number authenticated

by Face Authentication Technology on the UMANG Application.

The applicant must have gross wages up to 1,00,000/- per month at

the time of joining the establishment.

The applicant must have Electronic Challan-cum-Returns along with

contributions filed for six continuous months.
The applicant must continue with the same establishment for the entire
period of six months if they are employed in a seasonal industry.
Eligibility for Employees (Re-joinees)
The applicant must have a previous contributory membership of the
Employees' Provident Fund.
The applicant must join an establishment registered with the Employees'
Provident Fund Organisation or an exempted trust during the scheme
registration period.
The applicant must have a Universal Account Number, which is
Aadhaar-authenticated using biometric or face authentication
technology.
Eligibility for Establishments (Employers)
The applicant must be covered under the Employees' Provident Funds
and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952.
The applicant must have a unique Permanent Account Number.
The applicant must file the Electronic Challan-cum-Return along with
contributions regularly.
The applicant must generate additional employment meeting the
threshold of at least 2 additional employments if their baseline is less
than 50.
The applicant must generate additional employment meeting the
threshold of at least 5 additional employments if their baseline is 50 or
above.
The applicant must provide details of their Permanent Account Number,
Goods and Services Tax Network, and a Permanent Account Number
linked Bank Account Number.
þÿ
Exclusions
The applicant must not be an old employee who joined the
establishment before 01.08.2025.
The applicant must not have pending inquiries under Section 7A, 7B, or
7C of the Employees' Provident Funds and Miscellaneous Provisions
Act, 1952.
The applicant must not have a First Information Report filed against
them by the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation for fraudulent
practices.
The applicant must not have pending or decided inquiries relating to
irregularities under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Rojgar Yojana scheme.

Application Process
Online
Registration Process For Establishments
Step 1: The employer must access the official portal of the Employees'
Provident Fund Organisation to initiate the deemed registration process
for their existing or newly covered establishment. The establishment
must ensure its standard records are fully compliant with current
statutory mandates.
Step 2: The employer must update and accurately provide their unique
Permanent Account Number, Goods and Services Tax Network details,
and a Permanent Account Number linked Bank Account Number within
the portal to facilitate direct benefit transfers.
þÿ
Registration Process For Employees
Step 1: The employee must download and access the official UMANG
Mobile Application on their smartphone to begin the registration process
and securely generate a Universal Account Number for the scheme.
Step 2: The employee must utilize the Facial Authentication Technology
facility provided within the UMANG Application to authenticate their
identity and successfully link their Aadhaar to the new Universal
Account Number.
þÿ
Application Process For Benefits Disbursement
Step 1: The establishment must accurately file the monthly Electronic
Challan-cum-Return along with the prescribed amount of provident fund
contributions for all eligible newly joined employees and re-joinees
through the designated online return portal.
Step 2: The establishment must ensure the continuous filing of the
Electronic Challan-cum-Return for six completed wage months to
successfully trigger the automated six-monthly lump sum benefit
calculation and subsequent disbursement process.
þÿ
Post-Application Processes
Step 1: The first-time employee must log in to the Employees' Provident
Fund Organisation employee portal and successfully complete the
mandatory online Financial Literacy Course to become fully eligible for
the second installment of their designated incentive.
Step 2: The applicant can track their application and file any grievances
related to eligibility or non-receipt of incentives using the online
grievance redressal facility and escalation matrix provided by the
Employees' Provident Fund Organisation, ensuring resolution within
fifteen days.

Grievance Redressal / Help & Support
The Employees' Provident Fund Organisation provides an online facility
and a dedicated call center for the Pradhan Mantri Viksit Bharat Rozgar
Yojana to assist stakeholders and resolve grievances within fifteen days.
