Put your banner / poster images in THIS folder.

Name them:  banner1.png, banner2.png, banner3.png, ... up to banner8.png
(PNG or JPG both work — if JPG, rename to .png is NOT needed; edit the
CANDIDATES list in components/ui/BannerCarousel.tsx to use .jpg.)

They will scroll left-to-right across the top of the home page automatically.
Any missing numbers are skipped, so you can use as few as 1 or as many as 8.

Tip: wide images (e.g. 1500x1000) look best in the moving strip.
